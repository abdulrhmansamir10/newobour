Pill label for counts, feature tags, and metadata. For legal land states use `StatusBadge`.

```jsx
<Badge tone="gold" solid>موثّق</Badge>
<Badge tone="navy">ناصية</Badge>
<Badge tone="info" size="sm">بحري</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral | navy | gold | success | warning | danger | info`. `solid` fills the background; default is a soft tint. Sizes `sm | md`.
